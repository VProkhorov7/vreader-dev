## The Problem

KyBook 3 and Marvin — the best iOS readers ever made — are abandoned. No updates for 3+ years. Broken cloud sync. No AI. No future.

Millions of readers are left with apps that crash on modern iOS, can't connect to Google Drive, and have no idea what year it is.

**VReader is the replacement they've been waiting for.**

---

## What Makes VReader Different

### 📚 Every Format You Own
PDF · EPUB · FB2 · CBZ · CBR · DjVu · MOBI · AZW3 · CHM · TXT · RTF · MP3 · M4B · AAC

One library. All your books. No format left behind.

### ☁️ Every Cloud You Use
iCloud · WebDAV · Yandex.Disk · Nextcloud · Mail.ru · Google Drive · Dropbox · OneDrive · SMB/NAS · OPDS (Calibre, Gutenberg, Flibusta)

Books appear in your library automatically — cloud, preview, or fully downloaded. You choose.

### 🤖 AI That Actually Helps
- **Instant translation** of any chapter into 50+ languages
- **Natural text-to-speech** — keeps playing when you switch apps
- **"Catch me up"** — AI summary of chapters you skipped
- **X-Ray** — track characters and mentions across the whole book
- **Smart dictionary** — explains idioms, cultural references, complex terms

All powered by Gemini. All built into the reader — no copy-pasting, no switching apps.

### 🎨 Design Worth Looking At
Four carefully crafted themes:
- **Editorial Dark** — warm black, gold accents, serif typography
- **Curator Light** — cream parchment, morning reading mood
- **Neural Link** — matrix green on deep black *(Premium)*
- **Typewriter** — aged paper, red ribbon accent *(Premium)*

### 🌍 Built for Everyone
- Full RTL support for Arabic and Hebrew
- CJK fonts for Chinese and Japanese
- Interface in Russian, English — Arabic and Chinese coming

## Free & Premium

**Free**
- Read all your local books without limits
- Basic text-to-speech
- iCloud Drive sync
- AI translation (up to 500 words per chapter)

**Premium**
- All cloud connectors (Google Drive, Dropbox, Yandex.Disk, SMB...)
- Full chapter AI translation and TTS
- AI summaries, X-Ray, smart dictionary
- Premium themes (Neural Link, Typewriter)
- Unlimited cloud downloads
